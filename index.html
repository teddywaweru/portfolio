Data Science Projects

Spain Electrical Shortfall

The government of Spain provided a dataset showing the electrical shortfall of renewable energies against fossil fuel production. Included in the dataset were recordings of weather attributes for select Spanish cities. The motive was to determine if it was possible to predict future shortfalls based on the data, as well as gather insights to assist in future development of the country's renewable sector.
